About Me

I’m a bilingual teacher, fluent in Arabic and English, with a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science from the University of London and a Master’s degree in Education from UCL. I’ve been teaching in primary schools across Saudi Arabia, the UAE, and Qatar.

My qualifications include First Aid and experience working with SEN (Special Educational Needs) children for one year as a teaching assistant. I’ve also volunteered in primary schools while pursuing my undergraduate degree.
